## Marcus, 15 — The Rapid-Fire Reactor
The type of guy who's always mid-group-chat during lunch or post-soccer practice. Phone glued to his hand, always scrolling, always reacting. He's got maybe 30 seconds before the conversation zooms past him.
* Where he is: Android phone, school WiFi (which sucks), constant group chats
* What he does: Makes memes about teachers, teammates, literally whatever's happening right now. Real-time reactions only.
* His problem: By the time he opens ImgFlip and finds a template, the joke is dead. Needs to be stupid fast or he just sends a text instead.
* What wins him over: MemeBro opens, face gets detected,template picked ,text added and he sends. Done in under 20 seconds.

## Priya, 22 — The Creative Sharer
College student who uses memes like language. Discord servers, Snapchat, Instagram DMs. She actually cares about how her memes look. Picks the right font, nails the placement, vibes with the whole thing.
* Where she is: Latest iPhone, solid WiFi, always connected
* What she does: Makes memes of her friends for inside jokes. Puts actual effort in. It's a mini art form for her.
* Her problem: Can't swap a friend's face into a meme smoothly on mobile. Desktop tools exist but they're clunky as hell on a phone.
* What wins her over: Face swap that looks good enough to screenshot instantly, easy to edit text/image.

## Jordan, 17 — The Lurker Who Sometimes Posts
High school junior, mostly a consumer of memes, but occasionally something so funny happens in real life that he just has to participate. Then he immediately forgets about it.
* Where he is: Older iPhone, Safari browser, barely any apps installed
* What he does: Scrolls. Lurks. Posts maybe once a month when the impulse is too strong.
* His problem: Doesn't want to download an app or sign up for an account just to make one stupid meme. If there's friction, he's out.
* What wins him over: Progressive web app (no download, no account) right in his browser. Friendly, not intense. Frictionless or he ghosts.


## Devon, 19 — The Ironic Shitposter
College freshman who's posting complete chaos at 2 AM in Discord and niche subreddits. Runs a meme account with 4K followers built on intentionally terrible, "cursed" humor. Meme-making is his creative outlet.
* Where he is: Cracked-screen iPhone, dorm WiFi that cuts out, has an entire photo album called "meme fuel" (all cursed)
* What he does: Doesn't care about clean templates. Layers random images together, throws incoherent text on them, uses Comic Sans unironically, deep-fries everything. The worse it looks = the better.
* His problem: Standard meme tools are too polished. They auto-correct his typos, snap text to the center, and won't let him be chaotic. ImgFlip feels sterile.
* What wins him over: Upload his own weird images (or raid a "cursed" template library) ,drag text anywhere he wants ,cool fonts(possibly?), a deep-fry filter on it and export and flood Discord with 10 memes at once.
